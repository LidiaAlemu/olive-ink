ABOUT OLIVE & INK

Some people collect souvenirs from places they've been.

We collect stories.

Olive & Ink was born from countless conversations between two friends, Hazel and Lilac, who discovered that some of the most meaningful connections can begin with a simple question:

"What are you reading?"

What started as sharing book recommendations quickly became sharing pieces of ourselves. A highlighted sentence. A favorite character. A chapter that felt too close to home. A story that understood us when we couldn't quite understand ourselves.

Some books made us laugh. Some broke our hearts. Some changed the way we looked at the world. And somewhere between all those pages, a friendship grew.

Olive & Ink is our way of sharing that experience with others.

This isn't just a place for book reviews. It's a place for reflections, conversations, and stories that stay with us long after we've turned the last page. A place for people who read not only for entertainment, but for understanding. People who find comfort in libraries, meaning in words, and pieces of themselves hidden inside fictional worlds.

Here you'll find books we loved, books we questioned, and books that left a mark on us. You'll find essays, reading journals, favorite quotes, and perhaps a few thoughts we've never quite said out loud.

Most of all, you'll find two friends who simply love books and wanted to create a space where others who feel the same could gather.

So whether you're here searching for your next favorite read, looking for a thoughtful conversation, or just wandering through the shelves, we're glad you found your way here.

Stay awhile.

There's always room for one more reader.

— Hazel & Lilac
